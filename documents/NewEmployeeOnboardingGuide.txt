Document: Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the team! The initial onboarding process includes:

1. Introduction day with HR and training on internal tools (Slack, Jira, Notion).
2. One-on-one meeting with your team lead to align on goals and your 30-day plan.
3. Completion of legal forms and mandatory security awareness training (due within the first week).
4. Setup of internal systems: VPN, company email, GitHub access.

For any questions, please contact Noa from the HR team.
