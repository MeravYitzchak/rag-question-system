To protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of company data, all employees are required to follow the following IT security practices:

Use strong, unique passwords for all company accounts and change them regularly.

Ensure that all devices are encrypted and password-protected when accessing company data remotely.

Do not share sensitive information via email or unsecured channels.

Report any suspicious emails, phishing attempts, or other security threats immediately to the IT department.

Adhere to company guidelines when accessing and storing customer or employee personal data.

Failure to comply with IT security policies may lead to disciplinary actions, including termination of employment.
